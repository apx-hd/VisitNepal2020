Popular Festivals

Bisket Jatra
Starts in 2 days

Bisket Jatra, Bhaktapur's weeklong festival marks the ancient solar Nava Barsha (New Year) and is the only festival that does not follow the lunar-based Nepali calendar.

Chhat
Starts next week

Chhath is an ancient Hindu Vedic festival historically native to the Indian subcontinent, more specifically, the Indian states of Bihar, Jharkhand, Odisha, Uttar Pradesh and West Bengal as well as the Madhesh region of Nepal.

Losar
Starts next month

Losar is a festival in Tibetan Buddhism. The holiday is celebrated on various dates depending on location tradition.

Gai Jatra
Starts in 2 months

Gai Jatra, is a festival celebrated in Nepal, mainly in Kathmandu valley by the Newar community.

Holi
Starts in 3 months

Holi; is a popular ancient Hindu festival, originating from the Indian subcontinent.

Indra Jatra
Starts in 6 months

Indra Jātrā, most commonly known as Yenyā is the biggest religious street festival in Kathmandu, Nepal.
